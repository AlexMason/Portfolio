About Me

Technology has been my passion, ever since I touched a computer I knew instantly what my calling was. Whether it was playing my dads MS-DOS horse racing game or tinkering with the early version of the internet, I always found a home in front of a keyboard.

Over the years I have garnered experience with multiple facets of technology including hardware, operating systems, programming languages, command line interfaces, IoT devices, and mobile smartphones. My first deep dive into the technical world happened when I was 13 years old and played an online game called RuneScape. I have always had the knack to solve a problem myself, and my issue with this game was I couldn't cheat because everything was on the server side. My solution? Create my own server and connect the RuneScape client to that. So that is what I did and that led me down the path of learning Java, MySQL, PHP, HTML, and CSS. This thrust into the interworking of what drove technology led my younger self into even more challenging problems such as running dedicated servers, automatic deployment, SEO, and as always the drive to continue learning technologies.

So that leads me to now, I am an uncompleted technological encyclopedia with entries to be filled and knowledge to be scribed and have the same vigorous passion towards technology as my 6 year old self did back in 1999. And of this I yearn for more.
